Sample of away_team

The Away Team table looks at five different variables. Opp_Abbreviation: which is a three letter identifier for each team. Opp_Score: which is the total points scored by the team. Opp_Pace: which is the total number of possessions taken by the team. Opp_efg_pct: which is the field goal percentage obtained by the team. Opp_off_rtg: which is the offensive efficiency rating obtained by the team.
