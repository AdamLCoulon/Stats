Sample of home_team

The Home Team table looks at five different variables. Team_Abbreviation: which is a three letter identifier for each team. Team_Score: which is the total points scored by the team. Team_Pace: which is the total number of possessions taken by the team. Team_efg_pct: which is the field goal percentage obtained by the team. Team_off_rtg: which is the offensive efficiency rating obtained by the team.
