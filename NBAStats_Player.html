Sample of player_stats

The Player Stats table looks at five different variables from the leading scorer of the game. Player_Name: which is the name of the player. Pts: which is the total points scored by the player. Minutes: which is the total number of minutes played by the player. Fga: which is the field goal percentage obtained by the player. Fg3a: which is the three-point field goal percentage obtained by the player.
