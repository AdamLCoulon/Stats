About the Project

This project looks at the currents trends in the NBA. We focus on offensive trends such as points scored, pace of play, and number of possessions per team. I am looking to see if the game is switching to more of an offensive approach as rules continue to change and players adjust to new playing styles. The database consists of three tables: home_team, away_team, and player_stats. These three tables provide data on both the home team and away team as well as the points leader for each game. With this information we should be able to find trends in the game and continue to compare to past and future numbers to get an idea of how the game is transitioning.
